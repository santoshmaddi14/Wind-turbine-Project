# Wind-turbine-Project
Al - Driven Predictive Maintenance for Wind Turbines
Al-Driven Predictive Maintenance for Wind Turbines in Renewable Energy project focused on enhancing wind
turbine efficiency.
Aimed at reducing downtime and optimizing maintenance in renewable energy.
Applied LSTM for anomaly detection in time-series data Developed a Streamlit dashboard to visualize turbine health
and support maintenance decisions.
